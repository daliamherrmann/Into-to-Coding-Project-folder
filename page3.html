My Biggest Takeaways

Traveling abroad taught me a lot about myself and the world. Here are some of the biggest things I took away from the experience.

Things I Learned

  1. I learned how to be more spontaneous and go with the flow
  2. I learned how to communicate across language barriers
  3. I learned how different cultures approach daily life
  4. I learned how to be more independent and it was the first time I didnt have a dinning hall
  5. I learned how to step outside my comfort zone

Would I Do It Again?

Yes, I would definitely do it again. The experience was great and taught me so much about myself and the world. I would love to explore more countries and cultures in the future.
